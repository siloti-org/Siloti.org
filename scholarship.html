Scholarship on the life and work of Alexander Siloti is rapidly expanding. In Russia and beyond, musical scholars are more and more coming to recognize Siloti's role in ushering in a new musical era. The following bibliography collects Siloti primary sources, recollections, and scholarship. Siloti Symposiums provide an opportunity to engage, discuss, and support new research into Siloti's musical art.
